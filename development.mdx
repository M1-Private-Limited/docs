## Why build M1-GPT?

> Better GPT: Faster response time than public ChatGPT since requests are routed to internal APIs. M1-GPT has in-depth customization and contextual prompts.

> Access to Data Reservoir: Connect M1-GPT to our own M1 Data Reservoir to get tailored answers based on our M1 data.

> Cost Savings: GPT-4 is currently only available to 'ChatGPT Plus' subscribers ($20 USD/month). With M1-GPT, everyone can access GPT-4's x10 parameter model even without a subscription via a pay per use pricing model.

> Data Safety: Public ChatGPT collects and train users' data. M1-GPT's Azure Enterprise API will not collect and train any users' data.

> Cyber Security: M1-GPT is hosted on M1's Azure app service and requires M1 network to be accessible.
